Guess the word in 6 tries. Each guess must be a 5 letter word with one letter in each box. Hit the guess button to submit. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word. If the tile lights up green, the letter is in the correct spot. If the tile lights up yellow, the the letter is in the word but in the incorrect spot. If the tile lights up red, the letter is not in the word at all. Good Luck!
